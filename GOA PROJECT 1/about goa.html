About Us

GOA Academy was founded by Nika Keshelava, with the goal of teaching young people programming and technology skills.
The academy brings together motivated students and mentors who create a strong, supportive, and friendly environment.
The main focus here is practice, growth, and helping each other succeed.
At GOA, students learn programming, as well as personal development and teamwork.
Our programs are based on practical learning, mentorship, and teamwork.
At GOA, every student receives maximum support to achieve their goals.
